# Masterclass — CPG Distribution & Retail Operations

Read these four scenarios **in order** before attempting the hands-on tasks. They give you the domain foundation: how products flow, how data is generated, where we focus, and how we measure success.

| # | Scenario | What it covers | Time |
|---|----------|----------------|------|
| 1 | [Journey of a Product — Factory to Consumer](01-journey-of-a-product.md) | The 3 distribution paths, channel margins, why DSD matters | ~25 min |
| 2 | [Decoding a Field Merchandiser's Daily Report](02-decoding-a-sales-reps-daily-report.md) | Productive calls, AOV, OOS, planogram, the data trail | ~30 min |
| 3 | [Modern Trade vs General Trade](03-modern-trade-vs-general-trade.md) | Independent vs small-format chains vs large-format chains | ~25 min |
| 4 | [Distribution Metrics That Matter](04-distribution-metrics-that-matter.md) | Numeric Distribution, Weighted Distribution, the ND–WD gap | ~25 min |

**Total reading time:** ~1h 45m
